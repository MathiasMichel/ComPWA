Calculate fit fractions and its errors.

Calculate fit fractions. Fractions are calculated using the formular:

The complex coefficienct of the amplitude and the denominatior is the integral over the whole amplitude.

Parameters
parListresult with fit fractions for the single resonances
ampamplitude with the resonances which share the fit fractions
nSetsPrecise error calcucation using
nSetsMonte-Carlo events
