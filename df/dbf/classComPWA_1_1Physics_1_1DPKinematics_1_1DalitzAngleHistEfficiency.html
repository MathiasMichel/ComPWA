Detailed Description

Uses also TEfficiency object, but the variables are one invariant mass and the corresponding helicity angle. This avoids binning effects near the phsp boundaries. ATTENTION: We assume that the invariant mass of particle 2 and 3 and the helicity angle between 1 and 2 are used!
