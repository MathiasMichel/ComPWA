typedef boost::adjacency_list<boost::vecS, boost::vecS, boost::directedS, DecayNode> ComPWA::Physics::DecayTree::HelicityTree

Definition of our decay tree structure. We want a std::vector (vecS) as storage for fast access times and low memory consumption. However then the uniqueness of the nodes/vertices has to be ensured on our own (see addVertex() function). The ParticleStates are used as the vertex property values.
